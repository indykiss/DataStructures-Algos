Datapond

What is datapond?

As a trends researcher, I've noticed that knowledge sharing can be difficult across departments and geographies.

Because of this, a lot of time is spent answering the same questions over and over again.

For example, we're pitching a new bank client. Instead of a consultant gathering info about the consumer banking industry- something that 5 different project teams have done in the past 6 months- let's make one research package that encompasses trends in fintech, consumer behavior, spending patterns, industry disruptors, etc.

Datapond's goal is to create a repository of secondary research to accelerate the fact gathering process, so people can spend more time analyzing the data, instead of finding it.
